The Game Masters


Now, you may be wondering, “Just how did Elements come to be?” Well, here's each of our stories of how we ended up meeting each other and creating this masterpiece of a game.

Snowi

My name is Elijah M. Wadley, also known as Snowi. I am the creator of Elements and the world it takes place in. Since I was a kid, this was the world my mind has created. My ADHD and over-active imagination allowed me to create a world full of creatures, elementals, and stories for everyone living there. With the help and volunteer work of my Team, we are able to bring this universe to life in the style of an Open World RPG game! I am working on every aspect of this game with each team, and making sure we can make this game into something incredible. I met these people through discord servers and communities across the globe. This project has been a dream of mine since I was a kid. I hope you enjoy this game as much as I enjoy the world. Snowi's Words Of Advice: "Explore. EVERYTHING. There are many places, Easter eggs, and inside jokes to find that may make your experience just that much more exciting."

Nessu

Hello there, I'm Ryen J. Kao, who you'll know as Nessu. COME OVER IF YOU'RE INTERESTED”, and so I did, and have been hanging out ever since. As the lead writer of the stories you see within this project, I pray they are enjoyable to read through. Many of what shall be written stems from original characters of mine, certain members characters in the server, and of the game masters themselves. As well as hopefully, some evenly paced, but strong world building of Sosaku, (my writing is also pretty damn amateur, so judge it all you want). Jayden is my best pal, you'll see me hanging around with him more often than not. Of course, my main goal, is to blow up, and then act like I don't know nobody!
