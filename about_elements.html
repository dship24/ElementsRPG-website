What is Elements?


There's 10 Elements total, and they all have their own uses and subclasses. There's the element of Ice, you'll get hurt if you get too close to them. Don't play with Fire, because you'll get a burn that gets worse the more you touch the heat, and Water will make you slow and weak. Lightning is fast, but you should worry more about getting zapped than trying to watch it. Wind is more than a small breeze here, they can weaken blows, or strenghten them. The Plague is contagious, and if you're infected, you'll feel weak, and sickness hurts quite a bit. Life can and will restore those around it though, and Earth is strong enough to shield you from danger. Light will give you warmth and make you stronger, but Darkness is deep, and they'll take pleasure in your pain.
