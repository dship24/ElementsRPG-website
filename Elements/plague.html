The Infectious Element, Plague


This Element is a control type. Infected enemies will be affected in 2 ways. Every odd turn, they'll take 2 damage, and every even turn, they'll deal 2 less damage.


The Subclasses


Slick Striker: It starts off with your infect damage being increased to 3, but at it's final level, there will be a 25% chance that infect points will spread to other enemies, and your infect damage will be doing 4 damage per turn. Sick Surgeon: At starting level, it'll increase the attack debuff to enemies by 1, but when it's maxed out, your enemy won't be able to remove infect until it's fully stacked, and you'll gain attack for the amount of attack debuffs that are on your enemies. Slaughter Skin: As soon as you get it, you'll have a 25% chance to apply an infect point to the enemy that attacks you, and when this branch is completed, when you're attacked, your enemy will get a guaranteed infect point on them and enemies who are infected have a 25% chance to skip their turn.
