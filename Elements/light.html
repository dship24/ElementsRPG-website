The Radiant Element, Light


This Element is a mixture between a support and a control type. Their Elemental Boost is Blind. They can apply this to an enemy for that enemy to have a 10% chance to attack a different target then they were originally going to. That includes both their party members, and yours.


The Subclasses


Divine Acolytes: Your Elemental Boost will be replaced with a buff that grants a 10% damage boost and "Divinity" to an ally, which grants a 15% damage bonus against Unholy enemies. At max level, all of your allies will be doing twice the damage to unholy enemies. Shining Supremacy: Your Elemental Boost is replaced with a buff that grants an ally a chance to not take a point of an enemy's Elemental Boost. At max level, you can apply it to your entire team at a 50% chance, or apply it to 1 ally for guaranteed immunity, 2 times. Church of Light: If you choose this subclass, you'll have the choice to blind an enemy, or purify an ally, which removes any Elemental debuff. At max level, you'll be able to completely remove any Elemental Boost points on an ally and the chance for enemies affected by Blind to attack one of their allies is raised to 40%.
