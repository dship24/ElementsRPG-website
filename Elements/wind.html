The Zephyr, Wind


This Element is a Support type. Wind users can apply 2 kinds of buffs to themselves, though only 1 at a time. They can either give an ally or themself +2 attack the next time they roll, or they can apply a damage reduction buff to themself, which reduces the damage taken by 4 the next time they're hit.


The Subclasses


Wild Winds: At the start of the tree, the damage buff that you can get increases to 3, but once you finish unlocking the skill trees, you'll be able to choose 1 ally's die and give them +2 on their next roll, and decrease the roll of all enemies by 2. Ward Winds: At the very beginning of the tree, the damage reduction buff will jump from 4 to 6. By the time you reach the end of the tree, your defense buff will give the targeted player the option to hover, which allows them to not take any damage from close ranged weapons for the the next turn. The defense buff will go up to 12 damage that can be tanked. Wandering Winds: At the start of the tree, you'll lose the ability to buff, but you'll gain some passive AOE damage. Every 3 turns, your attack will do 25% of your damage to enemies that are adjacent to your targeted enemy. At the end of the tree, you'll have a 10% chance to have a 2nd attack that deals 50% of the damage of the previous attack, and every single time you attack, you'll be dealing 25% of your damage to enemies that were adjacent to that targeted enemy.
